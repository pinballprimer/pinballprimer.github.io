Magic Circle (Bally, 1965)

Game rules

There are 3 top lanes. The rightmost lane always scores 10 points. The left two lanes score 10 points, or 50 when lit; only one is lit at a time, and they alternate whenever a 50-point switch is scored.

At the start of each ball, the three blue mushroom bumpers across the center of the game are lit. Hit a lit mushroom bumper to unlight it, collect its number, and score 50 points. Unlit mushroom bumpers score 10 points. Collecting 1, 2, and 3 opens the gate between/above the flippers and lights the green mushroom bumper in the top left to collect 4. Collecting the 4 opens the free ball gate in the right out lane and lights the red mushroom bumper in the lower right to collect 5. Collecting all of 1-2-3-4-5 lights the left out lane for a Special, worth one free game.
Using the top gate, accessed by shooting between the center and right pop bumpers, scores 50 points, puts the ball back into the shooter lane for a replunge, and does not reset any features. Using the top gate also lights one horseshoe on the backglass; this is a carryover award that persists between games. Lighting horseshoe #10 resets the sequence and awards either 1, 2, or 3 free games.
Using the free ball gate in the right out lane does reset all collected 1-2-3-4-5 numbers.
The white mushroom bumper on the left starts each ball at a value of 20 points. This value is increased to 30 points by collecting 1-2-3, 50 points by collecting 4, and 100 points by collecting 5.

Pop bumpers and slingshots score 1 point, or 10 when lit. They are lit intermittently based on switch hits, likely using a dynamic difficulty system seen in many mid-1960s Bally games that causes features to be lit less frequently if the machine has recently given out a high percentage of free games.

There are no in lanes. 2-inch mini flippers back up directly to the slingshots. Out lanes score 50 points, regardless of whether or not the Special is lit on the left or the free ball gate is open on the right. There is no center post.

There is no end of ball bonus or extra ball feature. Tilt ends game.
