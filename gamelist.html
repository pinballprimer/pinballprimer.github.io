Rules and Strategies Pages

Below is The Pinball Primer's current selection of pinball tutorials. All games feature a synopsis section with only the most important strategies and things to know, followed by a thorough detail of all game features.
This site is a work in progress, and table rulesets are constantly being added. To ask a question or make a suggestion, request, or correction, use the contact info at the bottom of the main page.
Please enjoy!

Full table names are used in this listing. If a game isn't where you're expecting, be sure you're not leaving any words off the front of its title, such as "Red & Ted's" in Red & Ted's Road Show or the "James Cameron's" in James Cameron's Avatar. The only exception to this rule is when a game begins with the word The; for alphabetical purposes, the definite article will be removed.

Abbreviations:
EM = Electro-Mechanical
SS = early Solid State
AN = AlphaNumeric
DMD = Dot Matrix Display
LCD = Liquid Crystal Display (full colour television-style screen)
