King Kong (Data East, 1990)

Quick strategy synopsis

From the left flipper, shoot the back right ramp to access the upper mini-playfield. Spelling APE at the top lanes in this playfield awards a letter in King Kong, and spelling King Kong lights the Scanner saucer in the back right of the main playfield for Lite Everything, a King's Ransom-style mini-wizard feature. From either right flipper, shoot the plastic ramp on the far left and time your flipper press to earn 1,000,000 points from the missile launch. In multiball, locks are lit by completing the red-yellow-green standup target bank, and locks are made at the metal ramp in the back left; both of these are very dangerous to shoot at.

Shots and table features

Skill shot

The shooter lane slants slightly to the left. A full plunge will exit the shooter lane, go up the Ape ramp in the back right, and access the upper playfield. One letter in APE will be flashing and cannot be moved with flipper lane change. Making the flashing lane scores 100,000 points, and making either of the other lanes scores 25,000. If the ball goes through the A or P lanes, it will fall out a hole in the upper playfield and be dropped in front of the Kong flipper. If the ball goes through the E lane, it will be fed to the lower right in lane and lower right flipper.

It is possible to short plunge so that the ball does not go up the Ape ramp at all, but there's not really any reason to do this.

Left ramp: firing Missiles

Any shot to the left ramp in single ball play gives a chance to fire a missile by pressing either flipper button. The value of the fired missile is shown on the back panel of the game and the alphanumeric display, and can be 25,000, 50,000, 75,000, 100,000, 125,000, or 1,000,000 points. The value cycles automatically and higher values are lit for less and less time. The timing on this feature is consistent and anticipatable, so the 1,000,000 can theoretically be earned every time with enough skill.

Locks and multiball

Hit a flashing target in the red-yellow-green bank lights it solidly. Lighting all 3 targets in the bank lights a lock at the steep metal ramp in the back left. Hitting all three targets in extremely quick succession awards a Sweep Bonus, worth 25,000 the first time and increasing by 25,000 each subsequent time up to a maximum of 100,000 points. Locking 3 balls at the metal ramp starts multiball.
